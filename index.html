✦ Our Process

How We Create Magic

From concept to your jewellery box — every piece goes through our careful creation process to ensure you get the best quality and design.

01

Design & Sketch

Our designers brainstorm and sketch unique concepts, drawing inspiration from nature, architecture, and cultural motifs. Each design is refined multiple times before approval.

02

Material Selection

We source premium alloys, CZ stones, crystals, and pearls from trusted suppliers. Every material is tested for durability, finish quality, and skin safety.

03

Crafting & Plating

Our skilled artisans shape and assemble each piece, then apply multiple layers of premium gold or silver plating to achieve that luxurious, long-lasting finish.

04

Quality Check

Every finished piece undergoes a thorough quality inspection. We check for plating uniformity, stone security, surface finish, and overall durability before approval.

05

Delivered To You

Your jewellery is carefully placed in our beautiful gift packaging and shipped securely to your doorstep. Fast delivery with tracking for your peace of mind.
